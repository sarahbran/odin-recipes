Mango Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is my take on an alcohol-free mango pina colada. A mango pina colada is a tropical drink made by blending mangoes, pineapple juice, and coconut milk. The ingredients are typically blended with ice, creating a thick and creamy drink. Some variations may also include lime juice or additional sweeteners such as sugar or honey. It is typically served in a tall glass and garnished with a slice of pineapple or a cherry. It's a perfect drink for a hot summer day or for enjoying at a tropical- themed party.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 ¼ cups ice cubes
  • 1 ¼ cups pineapple juice
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Purée mango, coconut milk, ice, pineapple juice, cream, and sugar in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
